At Cafeteria TNC, we take pride in crafting unique and flavorful coffee creations that keep our customers coming back for more. From rich espresso blends to indulgent specialty drinks, our menu is filled with delightful surprises for every coffee lover

Cafeteria TNC's Classic Cappuccino

But what if you could enjoy these same delicious beverages from the comfort of your own home? With our DIY coffee recipes, you can do just that! We're excited to share some of our most-loved signature drinks, allowing you to recreate the Cafeteria TNC experience in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 1/3 cup of steamed milk
  • 1/3 cup of milk foam
  • Cinnamon or cocoa powder for garnish

Instructions

  1. Brew a shot of espresso and pour it into a cup.
  2. Steam the milk until frothy, then pour it over the espresso.
  3. Top with a generous layer of milk foam.
  4. Sprinkle cinnamon or cocoa powder on top for an extra touch of flavor.

Now that you have these DIY coffee recipes inspired by our menu, it's time to put on your barista hat and get brewing! Whether you're craving a cozy cappuccino, a refreshing iced treat, or a decadent mocha, these recipes will bring the flavors of Cafeteria TNC right to your kitchen. Tip: Experiment with different syrup flavors to create your own unique latte combinations!
